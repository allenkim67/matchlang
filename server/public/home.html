AMAZING FEATURES

We have a Language Exchange chat with the best tools.

Study Guide

Learn from your mistakes. Save a message and the corrections to your "Study Guide" by pressing the "".

"What does this mean?"

Click the "" then click on "What does this mean?" whenever you want help to know the meaning of a message.

"Is this correct?"

Click the "" then click on "Is this correct?" whenever you want help to know if something is said properly.

"How do I say?"

Click the "" then click on "How do I say?" whenever you want help to know how to say something.

Translator

Click the "" then click on "Translate" whenever you want to translate a message.

Make Corrections

Click on the message to type in a correction or ask a question about a message. The message will turn pink whenever you submit a question or correction. Click the message again to close it.
